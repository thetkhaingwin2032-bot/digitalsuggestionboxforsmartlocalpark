Sample Ideas

Green Garden

A community garden is a shared space in the park where local people can grow flowers, vegetables, or herbs together. It encourages community bonding, teaches people about gardening, and makes the park greener and more beautiful.

New Playground

A playground provides fun and safe activities for children, including swings, slides, and climbing structures. It encourages physical activity, creativity, and social interaction among kids in the community.

Recycling Stations

Recycling stations make it easy for visitors to dispose of waste properly. Having separate bins for plastic, paper, and other recyclables helps keep the park clean and teaches people about recycling and protecting the environment.

Green Garden

A butterfly garden is a small area filled with flowers and plants that attract butterflies. It adds beauty to the park, supports local wildlife, and gives visitors a peaceful spot to enjoy nature and learn about insects.

Mizu Nomiba

Consider adding a water fountain in the park that allows children to drink water or wash their hands after playing. Placing it near play equipment will help kids stay clean and hydrated. Choose a design made of metal or concrete with taps set at a child-friendly height.
